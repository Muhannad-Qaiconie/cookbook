    Components:

  • pieces of chicken
  • One onion
  • Three spoons of ghee or butter
  • Small spoon of salt
  • A teaspoon of black pepper
  • 4 pieces of cardamom
  • Three cups of water
  • A cup of toasted almonds for garnish

    • how to prepare:

  • Clean the chicken well, then wash it with water and add onions, salt, cardamom
  • Drain the chicken from the water after boiling it, but keep the boiled water
  • In a pot on the oven, stir the grits with ghee, add the chicken boiled water that you kept and a sprinkle of salt and black pepper
  • Cover the pot of grits until it is cooked
  • Pour the grits into a serving plate, garnish with pieces of boiled or grilled chicken as desired, and garnish with toasted almonds.
